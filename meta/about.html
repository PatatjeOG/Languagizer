Languagizer is a fun and creative browser extension that lets you invent your own secret languages and alphabets.

With Languagizer, you can create custom character mappings, save them to your personal vault, and then use them to automatically translate text on any webpage. Simply type !(your text) in an input field, and the extension will instantly convert it to your secret language.

Features

  • Create and edit custom alphabets with a mix of text, symbols, and emoji.
  • Save, load, and manage your languages in a local vault.
  • Import and export single languages or your entire vault.
  • A simple decoder to translate secret messages back to plain text.
